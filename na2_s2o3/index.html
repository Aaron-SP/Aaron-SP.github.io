Sodium Thiosulphate Calculator

This calculator specifies the amount of sodium thiosulphate needed to remove chlorinated compounds from your aquarium. This calculator assumes that the speciation of sodium thiosulphate is in the pentahydrate form. A safety factor of 2x is applied on the calculated output mix volume.
