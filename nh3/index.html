Ammonia Speciation Calculator

This calculator calculates the speciation of ammonia in water. Different pH and temperature affect the toxicity of ammonia in your aquarium. All forms of nitrogen in your aquarium are toxic to fish to some degree. Different species of fish may tolerate different levels of nitrogen in the water.
