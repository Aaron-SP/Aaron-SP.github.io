Carbon Dioxide Speciation Calculator

This calculator calculates the speciation of carbon dioxide and the carbonate alkalinity, A C in fresh water at atmospheric pressure. For reference, a total alkalinity level of 100-200 mg/L will stabilize the pH level in a fresh water body.
